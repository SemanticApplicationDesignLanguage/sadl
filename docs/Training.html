Training

Last revised 6/4/2021. Contact us.

Training in the use of SADL and OWL can be accessed in a variety of ways.

  1. The Topical Index includes both explanation and examples on many different topics.
  2. The tutorial Foundational Concepts in Semantic Modeling discusses the underlying concepts of semantic modeling with SADL and OWL in an incremental way with exercises and examples.
  3. Interactive training sessions are available from the following sources.
    1. GE Research: email aggour@ge.com.
    2. Natural Semantics, LLC: email andy@naturalsemantics.com. For an outline of typical training topics see Semantics Seminar Part 1 and Semantics Seminar Part 2.
