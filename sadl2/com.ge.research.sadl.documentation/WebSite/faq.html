Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. How do I cancel a build operation?
    An Eclipse build is cancelled in the Progress view. This can be made visible either by going to Window -> Show View -> Other and finding and clicking on Progress or by clicking on the small icon in the very lower right corner of the Eclipse window. When there is a build in progress, the Progress view will display red buttons to cancel the operations that are in-progress.
