SADL Xtext Grammar

Last revised 08/18/2014 by A. W. Crapo

SADL uses Xtext to define the language and provide language services such as parsing, hyperlinking, folding, etc. The SADL Xtext grammar definitions are available in Rich Text Format (RTF). Note that because of wrapping, comments that are on a single line sometimes show up on multiple lines in the RTF.
